How to Make Anzac Biscuits

Anzac Biscuits are traditional Australian cookies made with oats and golden syrup. They have a long history from World War I.

Ingredients:
rolled oats, plain flour, desiccated coconut, brown sugar, butter, golden syrup, bicarbonate of soda, boiling water
  1. Preheat the oven to 160 degrees Celsius.
  2. Mix the oats, flour, coconut, and brown sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Melt the butter and golden syrup together in a small pot.
  4. Dissolve the bicarbonate of soda in boiling water and add it to the butter mixture.
  5. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir well.
  6. Roll small balls of dough and place them on a baking tray. Bake for 15 minutes until golden.
