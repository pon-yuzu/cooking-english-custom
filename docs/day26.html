How to Make Chocolate Mousse

Chocolate mousse is a light and airy dessert. It is very popular in Australian cafÃ©s and restaurants.

Ingredients:
dark chocolate, eggs, sugar, heavy cream, vanilla extract, a pinch of salt
  1. Melt the dark chocolate in a bowl over hot water. Stir until smooth.
  2. Separate the eggs into yolks and whites. Put them in two bowls.
  3. Mix the egg yolks and sugar into the melted chocolate.
  4. Whip the heavy cream until it is thick and fluffy.
  5. Beat the egg whites with a pinch of salt until stiff peaks form.
  6. Fold the cream and egg whites gently into the chocolate. Chill in the fridge for at least two hours.
