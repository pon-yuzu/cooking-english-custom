How to Make Apple Crumble

Apple crumble is a warm, comforting dessert. Soft baked apples with a crunchy, buttery topping. It is easy to make and everyone loves it!

Ingredients:
apples, lemon juice, sugar, cinnamon, plain flour, butter, brown sugar, rolled oats
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. Peel and slice the apples. Toss them with lemon juice, sugar, and cinnamon.
  3. Place the apples in a baking dish.
  4. Make the crumble topping: Mix the flour, brown sugar, and oats in a bowl. Rub in the cold butter with your fingers until it looks like breadcrumbs.
  5. Sprinkle the crumble topping over the apples.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes until the top is golden and crunchy. Serve with vanilla ice cream or custard!
