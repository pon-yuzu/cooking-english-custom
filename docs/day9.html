How to Make Pumpkin Scones

Pumpkin scones are a Queensland specialty. They became famous because of Lady Flo Bjelke-Petersen, a former governor's wife. They are soft, sweet, and golden.

Ingredients:
self-raising flour, butter, sugar, mashed pumpkin, egg, milk, salt
  1. Cook the pumpkin until soft, then mash it and let it cool.
  2. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees Celsius.
  3. Mix the flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  4. Rub the butter into the flour with your fingertips.
  5. Add the mashed pumpkin, egg, and a little milk. Stir until the dough is soft.
  6. Shape the dough into rounds on a floured tray. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes until golden. Serve warm with butter!
