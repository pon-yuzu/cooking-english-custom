How to Make Rocky Road

Rocky road is a no-bake treat with chocolate, marshmallows, nuts, and biscuit pieces. It is easy to make and very popular in Australia.

Ingredients:
dark chocolate, milk chocolate, butter, marshmallows, peanuts, biscuits, coconut, icing sugar
  1. Break the dark chocolate and milk chocolate into small pieces. Put them in a bowl with the butter.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter in the microwave for 30 seconds at a time. Stir between each time.
  3. Crush the biscuits into small pieces with your hands.
  4. Chop the peanuts and cut the marshmallows in half.
  5. Mix the biscuits, peanuts, marshmallows, and coconut into the melted chocolate.
  6. Pour the mixture into a lined tray and press down flat. Refrigerate for 2 hours, then cut into squares.
