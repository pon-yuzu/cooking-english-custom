How to Make Waffles

Crispy waffles are a favourite breakfast in many Australian cafÃ©s. You can add many toppings like fruit, cream, and maple syrup!

Ingredients:
plain flour, baking powder, sugar, eggs, milk, butter, vanilla extract, a pinch of salt

  2. Whisk the eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla in another bowl.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
