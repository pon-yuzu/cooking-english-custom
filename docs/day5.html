How to Do a Tim Tam Slam

A Tim Tam Slam is a fun Australian tradition. You use a Tim Tam biscuit as a straw to drink hot chocolate or coffee!

Ingredients:
Tim Tam biscuits, hot chocolate (or coffee or tea), a mug
  1. Make a hot cup of chocolate, coffee, or tea.
  2. Bite off one corner of the Tim Tam.
  3. Bite off the opposite corner of the Tim Tam.
  4. Put one bitten end into the hot drink, like a straw.
  5. Suck the hot drink through the Tim Tam. The chocolate inside will start to melt.
  6. Eat the whole Tim Tam quickly before it falls apart! Enjoy the melted chocolate goodness!
