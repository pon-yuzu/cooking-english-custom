How to Make Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is a moist and delicious cake. It is very popular in Australian cafÃ©s, especially with cream cheese frosting on top.

Ingredients:
grated carrots, plain flour, sugar, eggs, vegetable oil, baking powder, cinnamon, cream cheese, icing sugar, butter, vanilla extract
  1. Preheat the oven to 170 degrees Celsius. Grease a round cake tin with butter.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Beat the eggs and oil together in another bowl. Pour into the dry ingredients.
  4. Grate the carrots and fold them into the batter. Stir gently.
  5. Pour the batter into the cake tin and bake for 40 to 45 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool completely. Mix cream cheese, butter, icing sugar, and vanilla. Spread the frosting on top of the cake.
