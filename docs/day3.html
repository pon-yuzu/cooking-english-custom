How to Make Pavlova

Pavlova is a meringue dessert loved in Australia and New Zealand. It is crispy outside and soft inside, topped with cream and fruit.

Ingredients:
egg whites, caster sugar, white vinegar, cornflour, vanilla extract, whipped cream, fresh fruit (strawberries, kiwi, passionfruit)
  1. Preheat the oven to 120 degrees Celsius.
  2. Whisk the egg whites in a clean bowl until stiff peaks form.
  3. Add the caster sugar slowly, one spoon at a time, and keep whisking.
  4. Fold in the vinegar, cornflour, and vanilla gently with a spatula.
  5. Shape the meringue into a circle on baking paper and bake for 90 minutes.
  6. Top the cooled pavlova with whipped cream and fresh fruit. Serve immediately!
