How to Make Vanilla Slice

Vanilla slice is a popular Australian pastry. It has layers of puff pastry and thick vanilla custard. Australians sometimes call it a 'snot block' because the custard is slippery!

Ingredients:
puff pastry sheets, milk, sugar, cornflour, eggs, vanilla extract, butter, icing sugar
  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Place two sheets of puff pastry on a baking tray and prick them with a fork.
  3. Bake the pastry for 15 minutes until golden and flat.
  4. Heat the milk in a saucepan. In a bowl, mix the cornflour, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  5. Pour the hot milk into the bowl slowly, and stir well. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook until thick.
  6. Spread the custard on one pastry sheet. Place the other sheet on top. Dust with icing sugar and chill in the fridge for two hours.
