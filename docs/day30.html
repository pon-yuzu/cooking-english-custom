How to Make an Ice Cream Sundae

An ice cream sundae is the ultimate treat! It is the perfect way to celebrate finishing 30 days of English learning. Let's make it extra special!

Ingredients:
vanilla ice cream, chocolate sauce, strawberry sauce, whipped cream, sprinkles, chopped nuts, a cherry, wafer biscuit
  1. Scoop two or three balls of vanilla ice cream into a tall glass or bowl.
  2. Drizzle chocolate sauce and strawberry sauce over the ice cream.
  3. Add a big spoonful of whipped cream on top.
  4. Sprinkle chopped nuts and colourful sprinkles over the cream.
  5. Place a wafer biscuit on the side and a cherry on the very top.
  6. Serve immediately with a long spoon and a napkin. Enjoy your masterpiece!
